8CREW

OVERVIEW

8crew is an online store focused on modern, limited, and fashion-driven sneakers with delivery across Ukraine.

The project represents a classic e-commerce platform designed to provide a smooth shopping experience on both desktop and mobile.

FEATURES

  • Product catalog with visual focus
  • Product detail pages
  • Shopping cart and checkout flow
  • User account and profile
  • Order history
  • Mobile-first responsive design

RESULT

  • a complete and scalable e-commerce platform
  • a clear and user-friendly purchase flow
  • a modern visual identity aligned with fashion culture
  • a solid foundation for growing online sales
